About.

Rock Paper Scissor Game

It is a basic rock-paper-scissors game, where each outcome from a round is totally random from the algorithm. Sorry, no AI has been used, just some random generated possibilites from the algorithm.
Hope you enjoy the game.

×

Rule.

The outcome of the game is determined by 3 simple rules:
•Rock wins against scissors.
•Scissors win against paper.
•Paper wins against rock.
If the outcome of the game is same then it is considered as a draw.
The first one to score five point wins.
